## ‚úÖ TN Visa Rules Validation

- [ ] **Total professions:** 63 eligible professions
- [ ] **Validity:** Up to 3 years, renewable indefinitely
- [ ] **Green card:** Can apply, but dual intent issues exist
- [ ] **Safer approach:** File I-140 first, then I-485 when ready

## ‚úÖ OPT Rules Validation

- [ ] **Standard OPT:** 12 months
- [ ] **STEM Extension:** +24 months (total up to 36 months)
- [ ] **Application window:** 90 days before to 60 days after graduation
- [ ] **Cap-gap:** Automatic extension for H-1B selected applicants

## ‚úÖ H-1B Rules Validation

- [ ] **Regular cap:** 65,000
- [ ] **Master's cap:** 20,000 additional
- [ ] **Total:** 85,000 per year
- [ ] **Lottery:** March registration, April filing
- [ ] **Max stay:** 6 years (extendable with I-140/PERM pending)
- [ ] **$100k fee:** Only for new petitions from outside US (most exempt)

## ‚úÖ EB-2 NIW Validation

- [ ] **Premium processing:** Available (45 business days, $2,805)
